ABOUT US

Seaport Alliance Limited is an established Canadian business incorporated in Vancouver, BC since 1996. We are proud of our 26 years of service in the areas of job placement and management consultancy. We have built established connections with organizations across Canada.Our mission at Seaport Alliance Ltd is to leverage our exclusive relationships with top executives to successfully integrate new immigrants into the Canadian workforce.
